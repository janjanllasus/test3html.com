-o HISTORY o-

Balagbag is a barangay in the municipality of Diffun, in the province of Quirino. Its population as determined by the 2020 Census was 1,796. This represented 3.20% of the total population of Diffun. ;

-o VISION o-

The constituents of Barangay Balagbag envision a community with a sustainable economic growth whose people are Empowered, Educated, Morally upright, Self-reliant, Imbued with a sense of well being and community-oriented living in a balance environment.

-o MISSION o-

To achieve the Vision of barangay Balagbag, a participatory responsive rational and Integrated development strategy will be pursued in order to create job opportunities and increase income of families: have an effective and efficient delivery of basic social services: increase productivity through improve support for agriculture, fishery, and forestry: promote sustainable use of natural resources and develop the human resources and infrastructure support for better future.

-o GOALS o-
